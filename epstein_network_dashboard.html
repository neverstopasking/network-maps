Click a name to jump to its dossier. The map and the write-up use the same IDs, so readers can navigate either way instead of rage-scrolling through a human centipede of paragraphs.

Method

This dashboard separates information into three tiers: documented, reported, and survivor testimony. Testimony nodes are included as open threads, not settled fact. The point is to preserve distinctions instead of flattening everything into one screaming blob.
